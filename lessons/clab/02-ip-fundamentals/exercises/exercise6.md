# Exercise 6

## What is the difference between a local and remote subnet?

The _local subnet_ is the subnet the host belongs to. Devices on a _local subnet_ can communicate at Layer 2.

A _remote subnet_ is a subnet that is outside of the host's subnet. Devices on _remote subnets_ cannot communicate at Layer 2.

## Why are gateways needed?

A _gateway_ is required for packets to travel beyond their _local subnet_. The _gateway_ is able to communicate at Layer 3 and connect _remote subnets_ to each other.
